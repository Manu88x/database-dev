### Brief Description of the Database and System

- The Ecommerce Product-App is designed to help K-13 Gear manage its online retail operations through a structured database management system.

- The system centralizes important business data including:

- Customer profiles

- Multiple contact methods (phone numbers and emails)

- Product listings and categories

- Shopping carts

- Orders and order items

- Payment transactions

- Shipping addresses

- Customers can register accounts, browse available products, add items to their carts, and place orders. The system records each transaction while ensuring that product price and quantity are captured at the moment the order is placed.

- Administrators and inventory managers can update product information, monitor stock availability, and track order payments.

- The database improves reliability by eliminating duplicate records, ensuring accurate product availability, and providing real-time order tracking. This helps create a smoother checkout experience for customers and better operational control for the business.
